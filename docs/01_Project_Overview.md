## Project Background
Businesses and content creators often struggle with managing multiple social media platforms manually. Posting content consistently across platforms requires time, coordination, and platform-specific customization.

## Problem Statement
Manual social media management is time-consuming, error-prone, and difficult to scale. There is a need for an automated solution that can generate, schedule, and publish content efficiently.

## Objectives
- Automate content creation using AI
- Enable image and video generation
- Support multiple social media platforms
- Provide scheduling and logging functionality

## Scope
Included:
- Content, image, and video automation
- Scheduling and publishing
- Logging and storage

Excluded:
- Paid ad campaigns
- Deep analytics and performance insights

## Target Users
- Small businesses
- E-commerce stores
- Digital marketers
- Content creators
